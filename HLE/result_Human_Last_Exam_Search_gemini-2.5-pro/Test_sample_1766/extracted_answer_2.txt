For a p-electron, the possible total angular momentum quantum numbers are $j=1/2$ and $j=3/2$.

For $j=1/2$, the common eigenvalues are:
*   $J^2$ eigenvalue: $\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$
*   $J_z$ eigenvalues: $-\frac{1}{2}\hbar, +\frac{1}{2}\hbar$

For $j=3/2$, the common eigenvalues are:
*   $J^2$ eigenvalue: $\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$
*   $J_z$ eigenvalues: $-\frac{3}{2}\hbar, -\frac{1}{2}\hbar, +\frac{1}{2}\hbar, +\frac{3}{2}\hbar$
