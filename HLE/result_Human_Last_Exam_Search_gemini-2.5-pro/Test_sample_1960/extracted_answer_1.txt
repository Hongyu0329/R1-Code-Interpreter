**Function `f`:**
Let `A` be the linear logic formula `⊥`. The function `f` maps a natural number `w` to the formula:
$$
f(w) = A^{\otimes w} = \underbrace{\bot \otimes \bot \otimes \dots \otimes \bot}_{w \text{ times}}
$$
For `w=0`, `f(0)` is the multiplicative identity, `1`.

**Formula `C`:**
Let `P_b` be the formula `(A^{\otimes b} \multimap \bot) \multimap \bot`. The formula `C` is the `m`-fold tensor product of `P_b`:
$$
C(W, m, b) = P_b^{\otimes m} = \underbrace{ \left( \left(A^{\otimes b} \multimap \bot\right) \multimap \bot \right) \otimes \dots \otimes \left( \left(A^{\otimes b} \multimap \bot\right) \multimap \bot \right) }_{m \text{ times}}
$$
Substituting `A = ⊥`, we have:
$$
C(W, m, b) = \underbrace{ \left( \left((\bot)^{\otimes b} \multimap \bot\right) \multimap \bot \right) \otimes \dots \otimes \left( \left((\bot)^{\otimes b} \multimap \bot\right) \multimap \bot \right) }_{m \text{ times}}
$$
