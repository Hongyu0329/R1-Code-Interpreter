For a p-electron, the total angular momentum quantum number j can be 3/2 or 1/2.
The common eigenvalues, listed as (eigenvalue of J^2, eigenvalue of J_z), are:
For the j = 3/2 state:
($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{3}{2}\hbar$), ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{1}{2}\hbar$), ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{1}{2}\hbar$), ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{3}{2}\hbar$)
For the j = 1/2 state:
($\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{1}{2}\hbar$), ($\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{1}{2}\hbar$)
