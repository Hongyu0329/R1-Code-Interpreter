$\mathbf{H}(x, z) = H_a\hat{z} - \frac{J_c d}{\pi}\left[\cosh\left(\frac{2\pi w}{D}\right) - \cosh\left(\frac{2\pi a}{D}\right)\right]e^{-2\pi|x|/D}\left[\text{sgn}(x)\sin\left(\frac{2\pi z}{D}\right)\hat{x} + \cos\left(\frac{2\pi z}{D}\right)\hat{z}\right]$, with $a$ given by the implicit relation $H_a = \frac{J_c d}{\pi} \ln\left( \frac{a \sinh(\pi w/D)}{w \sinh(\pi a/D)} \right)$.
