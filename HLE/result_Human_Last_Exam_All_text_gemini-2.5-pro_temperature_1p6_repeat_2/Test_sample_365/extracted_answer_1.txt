The product is a bicyclic compound containing a 9-membered ring fused to a 5-membered ring (a bicyclo[7.3.0]dodecane derivative). The 9-membered ring contains a ketone functional group and a (tert-butyldimethylsilyl)oxy group. The 5-membered ring contains a C=C double bond and a gem-dimethoxy group. This product is formed through an anionic oxy-Cope rearrangement.
