The formula for the fermionic partition function Z in the imaginary time path integral formalism is $Z = \int \mathcal{D}\psi^* \mathcal{D}\psi \ e^{-S_E[\psi^*, \psi]}$, where the Euclidean action is $S_E[\psi^*, \psi] = \int_{0}^{\beta} d\tau \left[ \sum_i \psi_i^*(\tau) \frac{\partial}{\partial \tau} \psi_i(\tau) + H\left(\psi^*(\tau), \psi(\tau)\right) \right]$, and the Grassmann fields $\psi_i(\tau)$ satisfy anti-periodic boundary conditions, $\psi_i(\beta) = -\psi_i(0)$.
