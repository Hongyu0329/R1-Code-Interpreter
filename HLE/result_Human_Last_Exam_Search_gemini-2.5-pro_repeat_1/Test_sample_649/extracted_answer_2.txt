The tightest lower bound on \(R_n^*\) that can be proven with the given information using a standard Le Cam's method argument is:
\[
R_n^* \ge \frac{1}{2} \Phi\left(\frac{\delta}{2}\right) \left(1 - d_{TV}\left(P_0^n, \frac{1}{N}\sum_{j=1}^N P_j^n\right)\right)
\]
where \(d_{TV}(P, Q)\) denotes the total variation distance between the probability measures \(P\) and \(Q\).
