\n**Function f:**\n$f(w) = \bot^w$, which is an abbreviation for $\underbrace{\bot \otimes \bot \otimes \dots \otimes \bot}_{w \text{ times}}$. For the edge case $w=0$, $f(0)=\mathbf{1}$.\n\n**Formula C:**\nLet the formula for a single "bin" or partition requirement be $P_b = (\bot^b) \multimap \bot$.\nThe complete formula $C(W, m, b)$ is then defined as:\n$C(W, m, b) = \underbrace{P_b \multimap (P_b \multimap \dots (P_b}_{m \text{ times}} \multimap \bot^m)\dots)$\nwhich, written out fully, is:\n$C(W, m, b) = ((\bot^b \multimap \bot) \multimap ((\bot^b \multimap \bot) \multimap \dots ((\bot^b \multimap \bot) \multimap \bot^m)\dots))$\nwith `m` nested implications of the term `(\bot^b \multimap \bot)`.
