
1. **The relativity of simultaneity**: True
2. **Relativity of lengths**: True (but it's length expansion, not contraction)
3. **Relativity of time**: True (but moving clocks run faster, not slower)
4. **Invariance of the speed of light**: False
5. **Non-Newtonian addition of speeds**: True
6. **Formula for length relativity**: $L = L_0 \sqrt{1 + v^2}$
7. **Formula for time relativity**: $\Delta t_{moving} = \frac{\Delta t_{rest}}{\sqrt{1 + v^2}}$
8. **Formula for speed addition**: $u' = \frac{u - v}{1 + uv}$
