The density profile \(\rho(z)\) is determined by the implicit equation:
\[
RT \left[ \ln\left(\frac{\rho(z)/\left(1-\frac{\rho(z)b_{\text{mix}}}{M_{\text{avg}}}\right)}{\rho(0)/\left(1-\frac{\rho(0)b_{\text{mix}}}{M_{\text{avg}}}\right)}\right) + \frac{b_{\text{mix}}}{1-\frac{\rho(z)b_{\text{mix}}}{M_{\text{avg}}}} - \frac{b_{\text{mix}}}{1-\frac{\rho(0)b_{\text{mix}}}{M_{\text{avg}}}} \right] - \frac{2a_{\text{mix}}}{M_{\text{avg}}}(\rho(z)-\rho(0)) = -M_{\text{avg}}gz
\]
where:
- \(M_{\text{avg}} \approx 0.03486 \, \text{kg/mol}\)
- \(a_{\text{mix}} \approx 2.947 \, \text{Pa} \cdot \text{m}^6 \cdot \text{mol}^{-2}\)
- \(b_{\text{mix}} \approx 0.0443 \, \text{m}^3 \cdot \text{mol}^{-1}\)
- \(R = 8.314 \, \text{J} \cdot \text{mol}^{-1} \cdot \text{K}^{-1}\)
- \(T = 500 \, \text{K}\)
- \(g = 9.81 \, \text{m/s}^2\)
- \(\rho(0)\) is the density of the gas mixture at the bottom of the container (\(z=0\)).
