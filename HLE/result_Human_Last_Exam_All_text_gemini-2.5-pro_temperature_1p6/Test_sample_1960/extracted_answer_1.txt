Let $A_0, A_1, \dots, A_{b-1}$ be $b$ distinct atomic formulas.

The function $f$ maps a natural number $w$ to the linear logic formula:
$$
f(w) = \bigotimes_{k=0}^{b-1} (A_k \multimap A_{(k+w) \pmod b})
$$

The linear logic formula $C = C(m, b)$ is:
$$
C = \left( \bigotimes_{k=0}^{b-1} (A_k \multimap A_k) \right)^{\otimes m}
$$
where $F^{\otimes m}$ denotes the formula $F \otimes F \otimes \dots \otimes F$ ($m$ times).
