The final expression for the infinite product is:
$$ \prod_{n=3}^{\infty}\left(1-\frac{z^3}{n^3}\right) = \frac{1}{\left(1-z^3\right)\left(1-\frac{z^3}{8}\right) \Gamma(1-z)\Gamma(1-\omega z)\Gamma(1-\omega^2 z)} $$
where $\Gamma(x)$ is the Gamma function and $\omega=e^{2\pi i/3}$ is a complex cube root of unity.
This can also be written as:
$$ \frac{8}{(1-z^3)(8-z^3) \Gamma(1-z)\Gamma(1-\omega z)\Gamma(1-\omega^2 z)} $$
