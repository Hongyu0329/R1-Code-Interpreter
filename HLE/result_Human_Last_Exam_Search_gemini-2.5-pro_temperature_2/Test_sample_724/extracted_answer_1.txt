Let `k` be the integer `(1 \le k \le n)` that satisfies the condition:
`100 * \sum_{i=k+1}^{n} \frac{1}{2i-1} \le m < 100 * \sum_{i=k}^{n} \frac{1}{2i-1}`

The maximum amount of water left is given by the expression:
`W_m = k \cdot 100 - (2k-1) \left( m - 100 \sum_{i=k+1}^{n} \frac{1}{2i-1} \right)`
