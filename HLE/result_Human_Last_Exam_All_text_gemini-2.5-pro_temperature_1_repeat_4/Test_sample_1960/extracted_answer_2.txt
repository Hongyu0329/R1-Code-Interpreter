
**Function f:**
$f: \mathbb{N} \to \text{Formula}$
$$
f(w) = \bot^{\otimes w} = \underbrace{\bot \otimes \dots \otimes \bot}_{w \text{ times}}
$$
(with the convention that $f(0) = \bot^{\otimes 0} = 1$)

**Formula C:**
$$
C(W, m, b) = \left(\bot^{\otimes b}\right)^{\otimes m} = \underbrace{\left(\bot^{\otimes b}\right) \otimes \dots \otimes \left(\bot^{\otimes b}\right)}_{m \text{ times}}
$$
