The expression for the magnetic field `H(x, z) = H_x(x, z) \hat{x} + H_z(x, z) \hat{z}` is given by its components:
$H_x(x, z) = \frac{\pi J_c d w^2}{D^2} \frac{\sinh(2\pi x/D) \sin(2\pi z/D)}{(\cosh(2\pi x/D) - \cos(2\pi z/D))^2}$
$H_z(x, z) = H_a + \frac{\pi J_c d w^2}{D^2} \frac{1 - \cosh(2\pi x/D) \cos(2\pi z/D)}{(\cosh(2\pi x/D) - \cos(2\pi z/D))^2}$
