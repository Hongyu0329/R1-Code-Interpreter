The final expression for the horizontal distance $D$ is:
$D = \frac{v_0 c}{g\sqrt{1 - v_0^2/c^2}} \text{arsinh}\left(\frac{g}{c} \sqrt{1 - v_0^2/c^2} \sqrt{\frac{2h}{g\sqrt{1 - v_0^2/c^2}} + \frac{h^2}{c^2}}\right)$
Or, in a more compact form using $\gamma_0 = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - v_0^2/c^2}}$:
$D = \frac{\gamma_0 v_0 c}{g} \text{arsinh}\left(\frac{gT}{c\gamma_0}\right)$, where $T = \sqrt{\frac{2h\gamma_0}{g} + \frac{h^2}{c^2}}$.
