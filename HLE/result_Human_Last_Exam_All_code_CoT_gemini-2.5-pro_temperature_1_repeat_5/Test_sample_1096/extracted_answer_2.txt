The optimal choice for the pair of step sizes $(\gamma_1, \gamma_2)$ is:
$\gamma_1 = \frac{4(\kappa+1) - 2\sqrt{2}(\kappa-1)}{\kappa^2+6\kappa+1} = \frac{(4-2\sqrt{2})\kappa + (4+2\sqrt{2})}{\kappa^2+6\kappa+1}$
$\gamma_2 = \frac{4(\kappa+1) + 2\sqrt{2}(\kappa-1)}{\kappa^2+6\kappa+1} = \frac{(4+2\sqrt{2})\kappa + (4-2\sqrt{2})}{\kappa^2+6\kappa+1}$
The order of $\gamma_1$ and $\gamma_2$ is interchangeable.
