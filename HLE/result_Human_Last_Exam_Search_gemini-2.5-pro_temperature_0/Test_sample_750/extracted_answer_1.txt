$z_{k,n} = \frac{i W_n(-(\frac{\pi}{2} + 2k\pi))}{\frac{\pi}{2} + 2k\pi}$ for all integers $k$ and $n$.
This set includes:
1.  The real solutions $z=1$ and $z=-1$.
2.  An infinite sequence of purely imaginary solutions $z_{k,0}$ for integers $k \le -1$.
3.  An infinite set of other complex solutions for all other pairs of integers $(k,n)$.
