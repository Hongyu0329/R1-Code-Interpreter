The best choice for the pair of step sizes $(\gamma_1, \gamma_2)$ is the set of the following two values:
$$ \frac{4(M+1) - 2\sqrt{2}(M-1)}{M^2+6M+1} \quad \text{and} \quad \frac{4(M+1) + 2\sqrt{2}(M-1)}{M^2+6M+1} $$
These can also be expressed as:
$$ \frac{2\sqrt{2}}{(\sqrt{2}+1)M + \sqrt{2}-1} \quad \text{and} \quad \frac{2\sqrt{2}}{(\sqrt{2}-1)M + \sqrt{2}+1} $$
