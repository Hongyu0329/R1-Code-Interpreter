The Lie group of infinitesimal transformations is generated by the infinitesimals:
$\tau = c_3$
$\xi = c_5 + \frac{c_4}{k_1} e^{k_1 t}$
$\eta = \left(c_6 e^{k_1 t} - \frac{c_4}{2} e^{k_1 t} x\right) u$
where $c_3, c_4, c_5, c_6$ are arbitrary constants.

The general representation for the transformations on x is given by:
$x' = x + c_5 s + \frac{c_4}{k_1^2 c_3} e^{k_1 t} (e^{k_1 c_3 s} - 1)$ for $c_3 \neq 0$
$x' = x + \left(c_5 + \frac{c_4}{k_1} e^{k_1 t}\right)s$ for $c_3 = 0$
where $s$ is the group parameter.
