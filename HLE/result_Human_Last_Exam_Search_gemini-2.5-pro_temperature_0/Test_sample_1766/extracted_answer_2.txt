For a p-electron, the possible values for the total angular momentum quantum number, $j$, are $1/2$ and $3/2$.

For the state with $j=1/2$, the common eigenvalues are:
-   $J^2$ eigenvalue: $\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$
-   $J_z$ eigenvalues: $+\frac{1}{2}\hbar, -\frac{1}{2}\hbar$

For the state with $j=3/2$, the common eigenvalues are:
-   $J^2$ eigenvalue: $\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$
-   $J_z$ eigenvalues: $+\frac{3}{2}\hbar, +\frac{1}{2}\hbar, -\frac{1}{2}\hbar, -\frac{3}{2}\hbar$
