The two problems the JTB definition runs into, ignoring Gettier problems, are the **Infinite Regress of Justification (Agrippa's Trilemma)**, which questions how any belief can be fundamentally justified, and the **Problem of Defining the Strength of Justification**, which highlights the ambiguity over whether justification must be infallible (making knowledge nearly impossible) or fallible (making the threshold for knowledge unclear and arbitrary).
