
1. **Relativity of simultaneity**: False.
2. **Relativity of lengths**: False (it would be length elongation).
3. **Relativity of time**: True (but clocks would run faster, not slower).
4. **Invariance of the speed of light**: False.
5. **Non-Newtonian addition of speeds**: True.
6. **Formula for relativity of lengths**:  $L = L_0 \sqrt{1 + v^2/c^2}$
7. **Formula for relativity of time**:  $\Delta t = \frac{\Delta t_0}{\sqrt{1 + v^2/c^2}}$
8. **Formula for non-Newtonian addition of speeds**:  $u = \frac{u' + v}{1 - u'v/c^2}$
