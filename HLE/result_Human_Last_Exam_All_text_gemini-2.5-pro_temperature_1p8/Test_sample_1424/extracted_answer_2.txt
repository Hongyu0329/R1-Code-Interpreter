The functional dependence of the bare Green's function $G_0(k, \omega)$ on the single-particle energy eigenvalue $\epsilon_k$ is of the form $G_0(k, \omega) = \frac{1}{\hbar\omega - \epsilon_k \pm i\delta}$. This is an inverse linear relationship, where $\epsilon_k$ determines the location of the pole of the Green's function on the real frequency axis.
