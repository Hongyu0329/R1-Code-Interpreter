The common eigenvalues $(E_{J^2}, E_{J_z})$ are given for the two possible values of the total angular momentum quantum number, $j=3/2$ and $j=1/2$.

For the $j=3/2$ manifold, there are four states with the following pairs of common eigenvalues:
- Eigenvalue of $J^2$: $\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$
- Eigenvalues of $J_z$: $\frac{3}{2}\hbar$, $\frac{1}{2}\hbar$, $-\frac{1}{2}\hbar$, $-\frac{3}{2}\hbar$

For the $j=1/2$ manifold, there are two states with the following pairs of common eigenvalues:
- Eigenvalue of $J^2$: $\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$
- Eigenvalues of $J_z$: $\frac{1}{2}\hbar$, $-\frac{1}{2}\hbar$
