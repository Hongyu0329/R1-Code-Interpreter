The common eigenvalues are pairs of ($J^2$ eigenvalue, $J_z$ eigenvalue).
There are two sets of states corresponding to total angular momentum quantum numbers $j=3/2$ and $j=1/2$.

For the $j=3/2$ states, the common eigenvalues are:
*   ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{3}{2}\hbar$)
*   ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{1}{2}\hbar$)
*   ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{1}{2}\hbar$)
*   ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{3}{2}\hbar$)

For the $j=1/2$ states, the common eigenvalues are:
*   ($\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{1}{2}\hbar$)
*   ($\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{1}{2}\hbar$)
