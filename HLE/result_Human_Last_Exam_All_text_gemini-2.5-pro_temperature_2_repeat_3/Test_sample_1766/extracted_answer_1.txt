For the states with total angular momentum quantum number j = 1/2:
The eigenvalue of J² is (3/4)ħ².
The eigenvalues of Jz are (1/2)ħ and -(1/2)ħ.

For the states with total angular momentum quantum number j = 3/2:
The eigenvalue of J² is (15/4)ħ².
The eigenvalues of Jz are (3/2)ħ, (1/2)ħ, -(1/2)ħ, and -(3/2)ħ.
