The formula for $c_k$, the exact amount of money that person $p_k$ gets, is given by:
$c_{k} = k \cdot \frac{n(n+1)}{2} \left[ \left(\frac{n(n+1)}{2}\right)^{2} - k \frac{n(n+1)}{2} + \frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6} \right]$
