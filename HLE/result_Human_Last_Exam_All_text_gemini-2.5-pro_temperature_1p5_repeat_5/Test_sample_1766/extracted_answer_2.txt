The common eigenvalues are pairs of ($J^2$ eigenvalue, $J_z$ eigenvalue).
For the total angular momentum quantum number $j=3/2$, there are four pairs:
1.  ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{3}{2}\hbar$)
2.  ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{1}{2}\hbar$)
3.  ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{1}{2}\hbar$)
4.  ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{3}{2}\hbar$)

For the total angular momentum quantum number $j=1/2$, there are two pairs:
1.  ($\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{1}{2}\hbar$)
2.  ($\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{1}{2}\hbar$)
