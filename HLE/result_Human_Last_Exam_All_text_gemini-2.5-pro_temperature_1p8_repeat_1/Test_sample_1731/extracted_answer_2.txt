**Equilibrium Mean Energy:**
$ \langle E \rangle = \sum_{j} g_j \frac{\epsilon_j}{e^{\beta \epsilon_j} - 1} $
**Equilibrium Entropy:**
$ S = k_B \sum_{j} g_j \left[ \frac{\beta \epsilon_j}{e^{\beta \epsilon_j} - 1} - \ln(1 - e^{-\beta \epsilon_j}) \right] $
(where $\beta = 1/(k_B T)$, $\epsilon_j$ are the energy levels, and $g_j$ are their degeneracies)
