Let `k` be the unique integer in `{1, 2, ..., n}` such that:
$$ \sum_{j=k+1}^{n} \frac{100}{2j-1} \le m < \sum_{j=k}^{n} \frac{100}{2j-1} $$
(Note: A sum where the lower index is greater than the upper index is taken to be 0).

The maximum amount of water left at the destination is given by the expression:
$$ k \cdot 100 - (2k-1) \left( m - \sum_{j=k+1}^{n} \frac{100}{2j-1} \right) $$
