The general form of the transformation on the coordinate $x$ is a composition of three fundamental types of transformations:
1.  **Translation:** $x^* = x + c_1$
2.  **Scaling:** $x^* = c_2 x$
3.  **Time-Dependent Translation (Galilean-type shift):** $x^* = x + c_3 e^{k_1 t}$
where $c_1, c_2, c_3$ are arbitrary constants representing the group parameters.
