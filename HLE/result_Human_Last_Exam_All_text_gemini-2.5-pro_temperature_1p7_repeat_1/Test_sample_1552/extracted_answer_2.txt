The second coefficient in the heat kernel expansion is the local expression $b_4(D^2,x)$, given by:
$-\frac{2}{3}\text{Tr}(F_{\mu\nu}F^{\mu\nu}) - \frac{13N}{120}R_{\mu\nu\rho\sigma}R^{\mu\nu\rho\sigma} + \frac{N}{15}R_{\mu\nu}R^{\mu\nu} - \frac{7N}{360}R^2 - \frac{2N}{15}\Delta R$
where $F_{\mu\nu}$ is the gauge field strength, $R_{\mu\nu\rho\sigma}$, $R_{\mu\nu}$, and $R$ are the Riemann tensor, Ricci tensor, and scalar curvature respectively, and $N$ is the dimension of the gauge group representation.
