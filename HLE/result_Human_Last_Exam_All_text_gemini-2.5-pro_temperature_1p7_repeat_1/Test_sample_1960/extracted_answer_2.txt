Let `A` be the formula `⊥ ⊗ ⊥`.
The function `f` is defined as:
$$
f(w) = \underbrace{A \otimes A \otimes \dots \otimes A}_{w \text{ times}}
$$

The formula `C(W, m, b)` is defined as:
$$
C(W, m, b) = \underbrace{B \otimes B \otimes \dots \otimes B}_{m \text{ times}} \quad \text{where} \quad B = \underbrace{A \otimes A \otimes \dots \otimes A}_{b \text{ times}}
$$
