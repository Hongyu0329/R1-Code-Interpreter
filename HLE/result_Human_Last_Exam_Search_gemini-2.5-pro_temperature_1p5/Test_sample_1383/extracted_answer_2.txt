**Achieved Doubling Rate (W):**
$W = \dfrac{3}{4} \ln\left(\dfrac{3}{4}\right) + \dfrac{1}{4} \ln\left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)$
Alternatively, this can be expressed as:
$W = \ln(3) - \dfrac{7}{4}\ln(2)$

**Decrease in Doubling Rate ($\Delta W$):**
$\Delta W = W^* - W = \dfrac{1}{2} \ln\left(\dfrac{4}{3}\right) - \left[ \dfrac{3}{4} \ln\left(\dfrac{3}{4}\right) + \dfrac{1}{4} \ln\left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right) \right]$
Alternatively, this can be expressed as:
$\Delta W = \dfrac{11}{4}\ln(2) - \dfrac{3}{2}\ln(3)$
