
**Function f:**
The function $f$ maps a natural number $w$ to the linear logic formula:
$$ f(w) = \bot^w = \underbrace{\bot \otimes \bot \otimes \dots \otimes \bot}_{w \text{ times}} $$
(Where $f(0) = 1$, the multiplicative unit)

**Formula C:**
The formula $C$, which depends on the parameters $m$ and $b$, is:
$$ C(m, b) = (\bot^b)^m = \underbrace{(\bot^b) \otimes (\bot^b) \otimes \dots \otimes (\bot^b)}_{m \text{ times}} $$
