The maximum amount of water left is given by the formula:
$ W_{left} = k \times 100 - (2k-1) \left( m - \sum_{j=k+1}^{n} \frac{100}{2j-1} \right) $
where k is the unique integer in $\{1, 2, \dots, n\}$ such that:
$ \sum_{j=k+1}^{n} \frac{100}{2j-1} < m \le \sum_{j=k}^{n} \frac{100}{2j-1} $
(The summation $\sum_{j=n+1}^{n}$ is taken to be 0).
