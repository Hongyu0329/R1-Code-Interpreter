The general representation for the infinitesimal transformation on x, $\xi(t,x)$, is given by two distinct forms depending on the parameter $k_1$:
If $k_1 \neq 0$: $\xi(t,x) = C_1 e^{k_1 t} + C_2$
If $k_1 = 0$: $\xi(t,x) = (C_1 t + C_2)x + C_3 t + C_4$
where $C_1, C_2, C_3, C_4$ are arbitrary constants.
