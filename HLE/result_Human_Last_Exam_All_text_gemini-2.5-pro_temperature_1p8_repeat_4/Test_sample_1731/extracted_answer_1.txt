Equilibrium Mean Energy: $E = \sum_i \frac{g_i \varepsilon_i}{e^{\beta \varepsilon_i} - 1}$. Equilibrium Entropy: $S = k_B \sum_i \left[ \frac{g_i \beta \varepsilon_i}{e^{\beta \varepsilon_i} - 1} - g_i \ln(1 - e^{-\beta \varepsilon_i}) \right]$, where $g_i$ is the degeneracy of energy level $\varepsilon_i$ and $\beta=1/(k_B T)$.
