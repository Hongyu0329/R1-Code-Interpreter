Two languages from distinct Asian cultures with no direct historical contact where the words for "mom" and "dad" are nearly identical or highly similar are Japanese and Turkish.

- **Mom:**
  - Japanese: **ママ (mama)** (used by young children)
  - The "ma" sound is a common feature in words for mother globally, though the primary Turkish word is *anne* or *ana*.

- **Dad:**
  - Japanese: **パパ (papa)** (informal)
  - Turkish: **baba**

The word for "broom" in these languages is not similar:
- Japanese: **ほうき (hōki)**
- Turkish: **süpürge**
