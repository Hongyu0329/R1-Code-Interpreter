The new food, likely containing velvet beans (*Mucuna pruriens*), is important because it is a potent natural source of L-DOPA, a precursor to dopamine. For a patient with a history of a psychotic disorder like schizophrenia, which is treated with dopamine-blocking antipsychotics, ingesting L-DOPA can dangerously increase dopamine levels and provoke a severe relapse of her psychosis.
