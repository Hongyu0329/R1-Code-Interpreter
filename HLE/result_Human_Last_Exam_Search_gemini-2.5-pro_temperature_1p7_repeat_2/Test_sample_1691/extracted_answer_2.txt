An approximate analytical formula for the integral for small $\epsilon$ is given by:
$I(\epsilon) \approx \frac{\pi}{5 \cdot 9^{1/5} \sin(\pi/5)} \epsilon^{-4/5} - \frac{2\pi}{5 \cdot 9^{7/5} \sin(2\pi/5)} \epsilon^{-3/5}$
This can also be expressed using the Gamma function as:
$I(\epsilon) \approx \frac{\Gamma(1/5)\Gamma(4/5)}{5 \cdot 9^{1/5}} \epsilon^{-4/5} - \frac{\Gamma(7/5)\Gamma(3/5)}{9^{7/5}} \epsilon^{-3/5}$
