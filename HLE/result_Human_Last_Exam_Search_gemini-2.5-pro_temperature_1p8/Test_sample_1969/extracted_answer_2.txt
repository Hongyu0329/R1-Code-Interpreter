For a fixed $n>1$, the exact amount of money $c_k$ that person $p_k$ gets is given by the formula:
$c_k = k \cdot \frac{n(n+1)}{2} \left[ \left(\frac{n(n+1)}{2}\right)^2 - k \cdot \frac{n(n+1)}{2} + \frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6} \right]$
This can also be written as:
$c_k = \frac{k n^2(n+1)^2}{24} (3n^2 + 7n + 2 - 6k)$.
