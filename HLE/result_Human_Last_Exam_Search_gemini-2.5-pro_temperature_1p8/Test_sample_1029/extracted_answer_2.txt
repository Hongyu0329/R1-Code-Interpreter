The Poynting vector is:
For $r \le R$ (inside the rod): $\vec{S} = \frac{\rho^2 v r^2}{4\epsilon_0} \hat{z} - \frac{E \rho v r}{2} \hat{r}$
For $r > R$ (outside the rod): $\vec{S} = \frac{\rho^2 v R^4}{4\epsilon_0 r^2} \hat{z} - \frac{E \rho v R^2}{2r} \hat{r}$
where $\hat{r}$ is the radial unit vector and $\hat{z}$ is the unit vector along the axis of the rod.
