The average area of a projection of the $n$-dimensional cross-polytope $P = \{x \in \mathbb{R}^n \; | \; \|x\|_1 \leq 1\}$, where the dimension $n=2k+1$ is odd, is given by the formula:
$$ \frac{\sqrt{n}}{\left(\frac{n-1}{2}\right)! \left(\frac{n-3}{2}\right)!} $$
Substituting $n=2k+1$, this can also be expressed as:
$$ \frac{\sqrt{2k+1}}{k! (k-1)!} $$
