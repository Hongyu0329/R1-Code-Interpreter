
Let $P$ be the formula $\bot \multimap 1$.
Let $Q$ be the formula $P \otimes P$, which is $(\bot \multimap 1) \otimes (\bot \multimap 1)$.

The function $f: \mathbb{N} \to \text{Formula}$ is defined as:
$$f(w) = P \multimap (\underbrace{Q \otimes \dots \otimes Q}_{w \text{ times}} \otimes P)$$

The formula $C = C(W, m, b)$ is defined as:
$$C = \underbrace{Bin_b \otimes \dots \otimes Bin_b}_{m \text{ times}}$$
where the formula $Bin_b$ is given by:
$$Bin_b = P \multimap (\underbrace{Q \otimes \dots \otimes Q}_{b \text{ times}} \otimes P)$$
