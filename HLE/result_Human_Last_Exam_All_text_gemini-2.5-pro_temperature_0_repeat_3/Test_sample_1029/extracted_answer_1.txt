For $r < R$ (inside the rod):
$$ \vec{S} = - \frac{E \rho v r}{2} \hat{r} + \frac{\mu_0 \rho^2 v r^2}{4\epsilon_0} \hat{z} $$
For $r > R$ (outside the rod):
$$ \vec{S} = - \frac{E \rho v R^2}{2r} \hat{r} + \frac{\mu_0 \rho^2 v R^4}{4\epsilon_0 r^2} \hat{z} $$
where $\hat{r}$ is the radial unit vector and $\hat{z}$ is the unit vector along the axis of the rod.
