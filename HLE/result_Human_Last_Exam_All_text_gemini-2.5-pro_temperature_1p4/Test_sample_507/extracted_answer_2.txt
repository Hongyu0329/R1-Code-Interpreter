The two languages are Korean and Persian (Farsi).
- **Mom:** The Korean word is *eomma* (엄마) and the informal Persian word is *mâmân* (مامان).
- **Dad:** The Korean word is *appa* (아빠) and the informal Persian word is *bâbâ* (بابا).
- **Broom:** The Korean word is *bitjaru* (빗자루) and the Persian word is *jâru* (جارو). The similarity lies in the Korean suffix *jaru* (meaning handle) and the complete Persian word *jâru*.
