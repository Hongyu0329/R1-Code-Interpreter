The Poynting vector $\vec{S}$ is given by:
For inside the rod ($r < R$):
$ \vec{S} = -\frac{E \rho v r}{2} \hat{r} + \frac{\rho^2 v r^2}{4\epsilon_0} \hat{k} $
For outside the rod ($r > R$):
$ \vec{S} = -\frac{E \rho v R^2}{2r} \hat{r} + \frac{\rho^2 v R^4}{4\epsilon_0 r^2} \hat{k} $
where $r$ is the radial distance from the axis, $\hat{r}$ is the radial unit vector, and $\hat{k}$ is the unit vector along the axis.
