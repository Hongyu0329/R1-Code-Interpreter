In configuration space, the inner product is $(\phi, D_\phi) = \int_0^\beta d\tau \int d^d\mathbf{x} \, \phi(x) (-\partial_E^2 + m^2) \phi(x)$, where $-\partial_E^2 = -(\partial_\tau^2 + \nabla^2)$. In momentum space, this is equivalent to $(\phi, D_\phi) = T \sum_n \int \frac{d^d\mathbf{p}}{(2\pi)^d} \, |\tilde{\phi}(\omega_n, \mathbf{p})|^2 (\omega_n^2 + \mathbf{p}^2 + m^2)$, where $\omega_n=2\pi nT$ are the bosonic Matsubara frequencies. This entire expression is equal to twice the free part of the Euclidean action, $2S_{E, \text{free}}$.
