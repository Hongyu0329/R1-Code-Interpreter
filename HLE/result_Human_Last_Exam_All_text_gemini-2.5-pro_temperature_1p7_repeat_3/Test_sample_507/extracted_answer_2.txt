Korean and Tamil.

1.  **Mom:** The word for "mom" in Korean is **eomma (엄마)**, and in Tamil, it is **amma (அம்மா)**.
2.  **Dad:** The word for "dad" in Korean is **appa (아빠)**, and in Tamil, it is **appa (அப்பா)**.
3.  **Broom:** The word for "broom" in Korean is **bi (비)**. A phonetically similar word in Tamil is **vii (வீ)**, which means "to fall" or "fallen petal," relating to what a broom sweeps.
