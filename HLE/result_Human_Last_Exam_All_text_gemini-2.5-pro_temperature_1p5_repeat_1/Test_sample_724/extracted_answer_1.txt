Let $k_0$ be the largest integer in the set $\{0, 1, ..., n-1\}$ that satisfies the condition $\sum_{i=1}^{k_0} \frac{100}{2n-2i+1} \le m$, where the sum is defined as 0 for $k_0=0$. The maximum amount of water left at the destination is: $100(n-k_0) - (2(n-k_0)-1) \left( m - \sum_{i=1}^{k_0} \frac{100}{2n-2i+1} \right)$
