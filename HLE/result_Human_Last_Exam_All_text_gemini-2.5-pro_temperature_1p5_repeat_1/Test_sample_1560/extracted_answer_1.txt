The final state of the system is probabilistic. Upon measuring one ququint of the entangled pair in the computational basis, the system will collapse into one of the five possible states: $|0\rangle_A|0\rangle_B$, $|1\rangle_A|1\rangle_B$, $|2\rangle_A|2\rangle_B$, $|3\rangle_A|3\rangle_B$, or $|4\rangle_A|4\rangle_B$. Each of these specific outcomes occurs with an equal probability of 1/5.
