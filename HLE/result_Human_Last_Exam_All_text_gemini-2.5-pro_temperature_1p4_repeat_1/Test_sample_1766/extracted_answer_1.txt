The common eigenvalues are sets of pairs (eigenvalue of $J^2$, eigenvalue of $J_z$).
For the $j=3/2$ states, the pairs are:
($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{3}{2}\hbar$), ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{1}{2}\hbar$), ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{1}{2}\hbar$), ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{3}{2}\hbar$).

For the $j=1/2$ states, the pairs are:
($\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{1}{2}\hbar$), ($\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{1}{2}\hbar$).
