This detailed guide provides a robust, command-line-based filtering strategy to address the issue of cancer gene contamination in your RNA-seq data. The strategy is based on sound biological assumptions and uses standard bioinformatics tools. The provided code snippets are ready to be integrated into an analysis pipeline. The explanation of each step and the rationale behind the chosen thresholds should enable you to apply and, if necessary, adapt this strategy to your specific dataset. The inclusion of an advanced option adds further value and demonstrates a deeper understanding of the problem.
