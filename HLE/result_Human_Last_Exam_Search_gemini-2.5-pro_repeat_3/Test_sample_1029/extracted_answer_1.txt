**Inside the rod (r < R):**
$$ \vec{S} = -\frac{E \rho v r}{2} \hat{r} + \frac{\rho^2 v r^2}{4\epsilon_0} \hat{k} $$

**Outside the rod (r > R):**
$$ \vec{S} = -\frac{E \rho v R^2}{2r} \hat{r} + \frac{\rho^2 v R^4}{4\epsilon_0 r^2} \hat{k} $$
where $\hat{r}$ is the unit vector in the radial direction and $\hat{k}$ is the unit vector along the rod's axis.
