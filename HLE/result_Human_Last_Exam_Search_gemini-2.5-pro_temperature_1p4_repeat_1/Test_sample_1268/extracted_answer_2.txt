An upper bound for the maximum norm $\|f\|_\infty$ of an $L^2$-normalized Hecke-Maass cuspidal newform $f$ with Laplacian eigenvalue $\lambda$ for the congruence subgroup $\Gamma_0(N)$ with squarefree level $N$, in relation to the covolume $V$ of the modular curve $Y_0(N)$, is given by $\|f\|_\infty \ll_{\lambda, \epsilon} V^{-1/6 + \epsilon}$ for any $\epsilon > 0$.
