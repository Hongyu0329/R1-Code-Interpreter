The normal cone \( T_F^°(x^*) \) is the set of all vectors \( s = (s_1, s_2, s_3)^\top \in \mathbb{R}^3 \) satisfying:
\[
T_F^°(x^*) = \left\{ s \in \mathbb{R}^3 \mid s_1 \in \mathbb{R}, s_2 = 0, s_3 \geq 0 \right\}.
\]
This can also be written as:
\[
T_F^°(x^*) = \text{span}\left\{\begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}\right\} + \text{cone}\left\{\begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix}\right\}.
\]
