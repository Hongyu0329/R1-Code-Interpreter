Equilibrium Mean Energy Density (u) for a Photon Gas:
Formula: u(T) = (8 * π^5 * k_B^4) / (15 * h^3 * c^3) * T^4
Result:  u(T) = 7.5657e-16 * T^4  (in J/m^3)
------------------------------------------------------------
Equilibrium Entropy Density (s) for a Photon Gas:
Formula: s(T) = (32 * π^5 * k_B^4) / (45 * h^3 * c^3) * T^3
Result:  s(T) = 1.0088e-15 * T^3  (in J/(m^3*K))
