The common eigenvalues are the pairs (Eigenvalue of $J^2$, Eigenvalue of $J_z$). There are two groups of solutions corresponding to the two possible values of the total angular momentum quantum number, $j$.

For the $j=3/2$ multiplet, the possible pairs of common eigenvalues are:
$(\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2, \frac{3}{2}\hbar)$, $(\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2, \frac{1}{2}\hbar)$, $(\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2, -\frac{1}{2}\hbar)$, $(\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2, -\frac{3}{2}\hbar)$

For the $j=1/2$ multiplet, the possible pairs of common eigenvalues are:
$(\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2, \frac{1}{2}\hbar)$, $(\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2, -\frac{1}{2}\hbar)$
