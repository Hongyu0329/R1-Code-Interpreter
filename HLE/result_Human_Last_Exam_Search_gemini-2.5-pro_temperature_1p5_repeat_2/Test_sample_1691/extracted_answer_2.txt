## Analytical Formula

The analytical formula that approximates the integral $I(\epsilon)$ for the small $\epsilon$ regime is given by:

$I(\epsilon) \approx \frac{\pi}{5.0 \cdot (9.0)^{1/5.0} \sin(\frac{\pi}{5.0})} \epsilon^{-4.0/5.0}$

$I(\epsilon) \approx C \cdot \epsilon^{-0.8}$
