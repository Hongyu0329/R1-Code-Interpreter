The best choice is the pair of learning rates $(\gamma_1, \gamma_2)$ given by the set:
$$ \left\{ \frac{1}{\frac{M+1}{2} + \frac{M-1}{2\sqrt{2}}}, \quad \frac{1}{\frac{M+1}{2} - \frac{M-1}{2\sqrt{2}}} \right\} $$
This can be written as:
$$ \left\{ \frac{2\sqrt{2}}{(\sqrt{2}+1)M + (\sqrt{2}-1)}, \quad \frac{2\sqrt{2}}{(\sqrt{2}-1)M + (\sqrt{2}+1)} \right\} $$
