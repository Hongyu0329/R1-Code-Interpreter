The Poynting vector is:
*   **Inside the rod (for $r < R$):**
    $$ \vec{S}(r) = -\frac{E \rho v r}{2} \hat{r} + \frac{\rho^2 v r^2}{4\epsilon_0} \hat{k} $$
*   **Outside the rod (for $r > R$):**
    $$ \vec{S}(r) = -\frac{E \rho v R^2}{2r} \hat{r} + \frac{\rho^2 v R^4}{4\epsilon_0 r^2} \hat{k} $$
where $\hat{r}$ is the radial unit vector and $\hat{k}$ is the unit vector along the axis of the rod.
