$$l_{\text{max}} = \frac{\left(Fl_0 + \frac{GmM}{l_0} - \frac{1}{2}Mv_0^2\right) + \sqrt{\left(Fl_0 + \frac{GmM}{l_0} - \frac{1}{2}Mv_0^2\right)^2 - 4FGmM}}{2F}$$
This solution is valid under the condition that $F < \frac{GmM}{l_0^2}$. If this condition is not met, the spaceship never stops moving away, and $l_{\text{max}} \to \infty$.
