For $r < R$ (inside the rod), the Poynting vector is:
$$ \vec{S}(r) = -\frac{\rho v E r}{2} \hat{r} + \frac{\rho^2 v r^2}{4\epsilon_0} \hat{k} $$
For $r > R$ (outside the rod), the Poynting vector is:
$$ \vec{S}(r) = -\frac{\rho v E R^2}{2r} \hat{r} + \frac{\rho^2 v R^4}{4\epsilon_0 r^2} \hat{k} $$
where $\hat{r}$ is the radial unit vector and $\hat{k}$ is the unit vector along the axis of the cylinder.
