The two languages are **Korean** and **Tamil**.
*   **Mom:** The Korean word is *Eomma* (엄마), and the Tamil word is *Amma* (அம்மா).
*   **Dad:** The word in both Korean and Tamil is *Appa* (아빠 / அப்பா).
*   **Broom:** The Korean word is *Bitjaru* (빗자루), and the Tamil word is *Viḷakkumāṟu* (விளக்குமாறு), both of which end in a similar "-ru" sound.
