For a p-electron, the possible values for the total angular momentum quantum number are *j* = 1/2 and *j* = 3/2.

For the *j* = 1/2 state, the common eigenvalues are:
*   Eigenvalue of J²: (3/4)ħ²
*   Eigenvalues of Jz: -1/2 ħ, +1/2 ħ

For the *j* = 3/2 state, the common eigenvalues are:
*   Eigenvalue of J²: (15/4)ħ²
*   Eigenvalues of Jz: -3/2 ħ, -1/2 ħ, +1/2 ħ, +3/2 ħ
