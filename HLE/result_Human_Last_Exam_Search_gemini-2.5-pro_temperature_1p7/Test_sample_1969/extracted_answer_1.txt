The formula for the exact amount of money $c_k$ that person $p_k$ receives is:
$c_{k} = k\left(\frac{n(n+1)}{2}\right)^{3} - k^{2}\left(\frac{n(n+1)}{2}\right)^{2} + k\left(\frac{n(n+1)}{2}\right)\left(\frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}\right)$
or written more compactly using $M = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}$ and $Q = \frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}$:
$c_k = kM^3 - k^2M^2 + kMQ$
