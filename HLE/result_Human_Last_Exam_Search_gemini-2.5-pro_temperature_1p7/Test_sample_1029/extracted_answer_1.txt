For $r < R$ (inside the rod):
$ \vec{S} = -\frac{E\rho v r}{2} \hat{r} + \frac{\rho^2 v r^2}{4\epsilon_0} \hat{z} $

For $r > R$ (outside the rod):
$ \vec{S} = -\frac{E\rho v R^2}{2r} \hat{r} + \frac{\rho^2 v R^4}{4\epsilon_0 r^2} \hat{z} $

where $r$ is the radial distance from the axis of the rod, $\hat{r}$ is the unit vector in the radial direction, and $\hat{z}$ is the unit vector along the axis of the rod.
