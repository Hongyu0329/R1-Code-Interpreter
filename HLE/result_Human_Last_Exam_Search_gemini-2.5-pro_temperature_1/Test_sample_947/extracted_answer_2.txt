For an infinite number of thin superconducting strips in the critical state under an applied field `H_a > H_0`, where `H_0 = J_c d/\pi`, the expression for the z-component of the magnetic field at a position `(x, z)` for `|x| \gg w` and `|x| \gg D` is:
`H_z(x, z) = H_a - \frac{2 \pi^2 H_0 w^2}{D^2} \cos\left(\frac{2\pi z}{D}\right) \exp\left(-\frac{2\pi |x|}{D}\right)`
