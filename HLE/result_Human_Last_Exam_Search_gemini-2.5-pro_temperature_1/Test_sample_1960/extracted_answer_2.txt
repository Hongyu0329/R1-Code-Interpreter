
**Function f:**
Let $A$ be a base formula, for instance $A = \mathbf{1} \multimap \bot$.
The function $f: \mathbb{N} \to \text{Formula}$ is defined by:
$$
f(w) = A^{\otimes w} = \underbrace{A \otimes A \otimes \dots \otimes A}_{w \text{ times}}
$$
(with $f(0) = \mathbf{1}$)

**Formula C:**
The formula $C = C(m, b)$ is defined by:
$$
C(m, b) = (f(b))^{\otimes m} = \underbrace{f(b) \otimes f(b) \otimes \dots \otimes f(b)}_{m \text{ times}} = \underbrace{(A^{\otimes b}) \otimes (A^{\otimes b}) \otimes \dots \otimes (A^{\otimes b})}_{m \text{ times}}
$$
