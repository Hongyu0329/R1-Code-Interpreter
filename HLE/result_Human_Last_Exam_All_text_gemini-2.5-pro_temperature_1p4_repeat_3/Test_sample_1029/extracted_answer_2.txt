The Poynting vector is given by $\vec{S} = -\frac{E \rho v r}{2} \hat{r} + \frac{\rho^2 v r^2}{4 \epsilon_0} \hat{k}$ for $r < R$ (inside the rod) and $\vec{S} = -\frac{E \rho v R^2}{2r} \hat{r} + \frac{\rho^2 v R^4}{4 \epsilon_0 r^2} \hat{k}$ for $r > R$ (outside the rod), where $r$ is the radial distance from the axis, $\hat{r}$ is the radial unit vector, and $\hat{k}$ is the unit vector along the axis.
