The most important counseling recommendation is regarding the drug interaction between Fluoxetine and the aspirin component of Excedrin. The pharmacist should advise Allison that combining an SSRI (Fluoxetine) with an NSAID (Aspirin) significantly increases the risk of bleeding, especially in the stomach. For future headaches, she should be advised to use a safer alternative like acetaminophen (Tylenol) and avoid products containing aspirin or other NSAIDs like ibuprofen.
