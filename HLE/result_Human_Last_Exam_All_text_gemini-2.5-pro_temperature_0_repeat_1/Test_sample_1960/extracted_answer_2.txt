
**Function f:**
Let the formula `A` be `1 \multimap \bot`.
The function `f` maps a natural number `w` to the formula `f(w)` defined as:
$$
f(w) = A^{\otimes w} = \underbrace{A \otimes A \otimes \dots \otimes A}_{w \text{ times}}
$$
For `w=0`, `f(0) = 1`. For `w=1`, `f(1) = A`.

**Formula C:**
Let the formula `A` be `1 \multimap \bot`.
Let `H` be the formula `A^{\otimes b} \multimap A`.
Let `G` be the formula `A^{\otimes m}`.
The formula `C = C(W, m, b)` is defined as the `m`-fold nested implication:
$$
C = \underbrace{H \multimap (H \multimap \dots (H \multimap}_{m \text{ times}} G)\dots)
$$
Explicitly, this is:
$$
C = \left(A^{\otimes b} \multimap A\right) \multimap \left( \left(A^{\otimes b} \multimap A\right) \multimap \dots \left( \left(A^{\otimes b} \multimap A\right) \multimap A^{\otimes m} \right) \dots \right)
$$
where `A = 1 \multimap \bot`.
