Let `k` be the smallest integer satisfying  `m < 100 \sum_{i=1}^{k} \frac{1}{2n - 2i + 1}`. The maximum amount of water left, `W_left`, is given by:
`W_left = 100(n-k+1) - \left(m - 100 \sum_{i=1}^{k-1} \frac{1}{2n-2i+1}\right) (2n-2k+1)`
For `k=1`, the summation term `\sum_{i=1}^{0}` is taken to be 0.
