The common eigenvalues for the $J^2$ operator are $\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$ and $\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$. The corresponding common eigenvalues for the $J_z$ operator are $\{\frac{3}{2}\hbar, \frac{1}{2}\hbar, -\frac{1}{2}\hbar, -\frac{3}{2}\hbar\}$ for $j=3/2$, and $\{\frac{1}{2}\hbar, -\frac{1}{2}\hbar\}$ for $j=1/2$.
