For a p-electron with spin-orbit coupling, there are two possible sets of common eigenvalues for $J^2$ and $J_z$.

For the state with total angular momentum quantum number **j = 3/2**, the eigenvalues are:
*   $J^2$ eigenvalue: **(15/4)ħ²**
*   $J_z$ eigenvalues: **(3/2)ħ, (1/2)ħ, (-1/2)ħ, (-3/2)ħ**

For the state with total angular momentum quantum number **j = 1/2**, the eigenvalues are:
*   $J^2$ eigenvalue: **(3/4)ħ²**
*   $J_z$ eigenvalues: **(1/2)ħ, (-1/2)ħ**
