Let $A$ be the formula $1 \multimap \bot$.
For any formula $F$ and natural number $n \geq 1$, we define $F^n$ as the formula $F \otimes F \otimes \dots \otimes F$ (n times). We define $F^0 = 1$.

The function $f: \mathbb{N} \to \text{Formula}$ is defined as:
$$
f(w) = A^w = (1 \multimap \bot)^w
$$

The linear logic formula $C = C(W, m, b)$ is defined as:
$$
C = (A^b)^m = ((1 \multimap \bot)^b)^m
$$
