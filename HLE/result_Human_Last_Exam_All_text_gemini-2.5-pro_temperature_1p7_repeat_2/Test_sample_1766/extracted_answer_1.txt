The common eigenvalues are found by pairing the $J^2$ eigenvalue with the possible $J_z$ eigenvalues for each total angular momentum state $j$.
For the states with $j=1/2$:
The $J^2$ eigenvalue is $\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$.
The $J_z$ eigenvalues are $\frac{1}{2}\hbar$ and $-\frac{1}{2}\hbar$.

For the states with $j=3/2$:
The $J^2$ eigenvalue is $\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$.
The $J_z$ eigenvalues are $\frac{3}{2}\hbar, \frac{1}{2}\hbar, -\frac{1}{2}\hbar, -\frac{3}{2}\hbar$.
