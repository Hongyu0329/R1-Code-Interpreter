The final state of the system is probabilistic. After applying gate Q to the first ququint of the entangled pair and measuring it, the system collapses to one of five possible states with different probabilities:
1.  (Prob 1/5): $|0\rangle_A \otimes \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(|1\rangle_B+|3\rangle_B)$
2.  (Prob 1/5): $|1\rangle_A \otimes \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(|0\rangle_B+|2\rangle_B)$
3.  (Prob 3/10): $|2\rangle_A \otimes \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}(|0\rangle_B+|3\rangle_B+|4\rangle_B)$
4.  (Prob 1/5): $|3\rangle_A \otimes \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(|1\rangle_B+|4\rangle_B)$
5.  (Prob 1/10): $|4\rangle_A \otimes |2\rangle_B$
