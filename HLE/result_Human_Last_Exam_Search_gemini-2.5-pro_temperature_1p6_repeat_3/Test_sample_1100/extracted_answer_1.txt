The new food, which tastes like bean salad, is important because it is likely rich in fava beans (broad beans), a natural and significant source of levodopa (L-DOPA). L-DOPA is the precursor to dopamine. The woman is likely consuming this food to replace the effects of the recently withdrawn dopamine agonist medication, which may have been controlling high prolactin levels (hyperprolactinemia) or treating other symptoms related to her psychiatric condition.
