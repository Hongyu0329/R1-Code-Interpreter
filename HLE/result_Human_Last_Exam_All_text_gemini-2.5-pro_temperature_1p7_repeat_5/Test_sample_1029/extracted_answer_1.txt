In cylindrical coordinates $(r, \phi, z)$ with the z-axis along the rod's axis, the Poynting vector $\vec{S}$ is:
For inside the rod ($r < R$):
$$ \vec{S}(r < R) = -\frac{E \rho v r}{2} \hat{r} + \frac{\rho^2 v r^2}{4\epsilon_0} \hat{z} $$
For outside the rod ($r > R$):
$$ \vec{S}(r > R) = -\frac{E \rho v R^2}{2r} \hat{r} + \frac{\rho^2 v R^4}{4\epsilon_0 r^2} \hat{z} $$
