For a Gaussian IT3 MF with fixed mean `c` and uncertain standard deviation `σ` defined by linear bounds `_σ(α) = (_σ_U - _σ_L)α + _σ_L` and `¯σ(α) = (¯σ_U - ¯σ_L)α + ¯σ_L`, the vertical cross-section at a fixed point `x` is an IT2 MF defined over the primary membership domain `u`. Its UMF, `¯μ_{V(x)}(u)`, and LMF, `_μ_{V(x)}(u)`, are given by:
`¯μ_{V(x)}(u) = ( \sqrt{\frac{-(x-c)^2}{2\ln(u)}} - \underline{\sigma}_L ) / ( \underline{\sigma}_U - \underline{\sigma}_L )`
`_μ_{V(x)}(u) = ( \sqrt{\frac{-(x-c)^2}{2\ln(u)}} - \overline{\sigma}_L ) / ( \overline{\sigma}_U - \overline{\sigma}_L )`
