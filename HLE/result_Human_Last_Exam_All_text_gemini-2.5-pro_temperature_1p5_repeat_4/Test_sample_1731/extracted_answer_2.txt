
Equilibrium Mean Energy:
General form: $E = \sum_i \frac{g_i \epsilon_i}{e^{\epsilon_i/k_B T} - 1}$
For blackbody radiation: $E = \frac{\pi^2 V (k_B T)^4}{15 (\hbar c)^3}$

Equilibrium Entropy:
General form: $S = \frac{E}{T} - k_B \sum_i g_i \log\left(1 - e^{-\epsilon_i/k_B T}\right)$
For blackbody radiation: $S = \frac{4 \pi^2 V k_B^4}{45 (\hbar c)^3} T^3$
