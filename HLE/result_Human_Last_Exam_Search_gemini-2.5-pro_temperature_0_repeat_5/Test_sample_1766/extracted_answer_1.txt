For a p-electron with spin-orbit coupling, there are two possible values for the total angular momentum quantum number, j: 3/2 and 1/2.

For j = 3/2, the common eigenvalues are:
- J² eigenvalue: (15/4)ħ²
- Jz eigenvalues: (3/2)ħ, (1/2)ħ, (-1/2)ħ, (-3/2)ħ

For j = 1/2, the common eigenvalues are:
- J² eigenvalue: (3/4)ħ²
- Jz eigenvalues: (1/2)ħ, (-1/2)ħ
