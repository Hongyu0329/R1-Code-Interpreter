**z-component:**
`H_z(x, z) = H_a - sgn(x) * [ (2 * J_c * d / \pi) * sinh(\pi w / D) / cosh(\pi^2 * H_a / (J_c * d)) ] * exp(-\pi|x|/D) * cos(\pi z / D)`

**x-component:**
`H_x(x, z) = -sgn(x) * [ (2 * J_c * d / \pi) * sinh(\pi w / D) / cosh(\pi^2 * H_a / (J_c * d)) ] * exp(-\pi|x|/D) * sin(\pi z / D)`
