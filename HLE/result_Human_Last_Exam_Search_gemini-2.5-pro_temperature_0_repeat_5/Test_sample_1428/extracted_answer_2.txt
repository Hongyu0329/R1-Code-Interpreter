The one-step transformation is $p_i(t+1) = \frac{1}{2} p_{i-1}(t) + \frac{1}{2} p_{i+1}(t)$. The transition matrix is $\hat{A}_{ij} = \frac{1}{2}(\delta_{i, j-1} + \delta_{i, j+1})$, with indices modulo N. The eigenvectors are $e^{ijk_n}$ with eigenvalues $\lambda_n = \cos(k_n)$ for $k_n = \frac{2\pi n}{N}$. The rate of relaxation is determined by the second-largest eigenvalue $\lambda_1 = \cos(\frac{2\pi}{N})$ and is given by the spectral gap: $1 - \cos(\frac{2\pi}{N})$.
