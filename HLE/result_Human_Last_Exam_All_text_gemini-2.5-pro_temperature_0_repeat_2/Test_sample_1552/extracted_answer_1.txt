The second coefficient is $A_2(D^2) = \int d^4x \sqrt{g} \left[ N_V \left( \frac{25}{72}R^2 - \frac{1}{45}R_{\mu\nu}R^{\mu\nu} - \frac{13}{90}R_{\mu\nu\rho\sigma}R^{\mu\nu\rho\sigma} \right) - \frac{2}{3} \text{tr}_V(F_{\mu\nu}F^{\mu\nu}) \right]$, where $N_V$ is the dimension of the gauge group representation, $R$, $R_{\mu\nu}$, and $R_{\mu\nu\rho\sigma}$ are the scalar, Ricci, and Riemann curvature tensors respectively, and $F_{\mu\nu}$ is the gauge field strength.
