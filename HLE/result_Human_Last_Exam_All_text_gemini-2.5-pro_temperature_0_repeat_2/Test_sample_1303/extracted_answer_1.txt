The small molecules that interact with CHEMBL4803817 (SARS-CoV-2 main protease) include:
1.  **Nirmatrelvir (CHEMBL4523589):** The active component of the drug Paxlovid, a potent covalent inhibitor.
2.  **Ensitrelvir (CHEMBL5077001):** The active component of the drug Xocova, a potent non-covalent inhibitor.
3.  **PF-00835231 (CHEMBL4296801):** An intravenous clinical candidate and a precursor to Nirmatrelvir.
4.  **GC-376 (CHEMBL4205519):** A broad-spectrum coronavirus protease inhibitor used as a research compound.
5.  **Boceprevir (CHEMBL162):** A repurposed Hepatitis C drug that also inhibits the SARS-CoV-2 main protease.
