In the poem 'Aqeedat al-'Awaam', the names of the Prophets are mentioned from the sixteenth bayt, which begins "هُمْ آدَمٌ إِدْرِيسُ نُوحٌ هُودُ مَعْ..." (Hum Ādamun Idrīsu Nūḥun Hūdu maʿ...), to the nineteenth bayt, which ends with "...عِيسَى وَطَهَ خَاتَمٌ دَعْ غَيَّا" (...ʿĪsā wa Ṭāhā khātamun daʿ ghayyā).
