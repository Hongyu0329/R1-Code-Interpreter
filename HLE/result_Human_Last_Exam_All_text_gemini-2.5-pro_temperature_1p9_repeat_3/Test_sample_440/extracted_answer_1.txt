The density profile `ρ(z)` is the sum of an ideal gas term and a correction term: `ρ(z) = (m_A n_A^0 e^{-m_A g z / k_B T} + m_B n_B^0 e^{-m_B g z / k_B T}) + \frac{2}{k_B T} [ m_A a'_{AA} (n_A^0)^2 e^{-2 m_A g z / k_B T} + m_B a'_{BB} (n_B^0)^2 e^{-2 m_B g z / k_B T} + (m_A + m_B) a'_{AB} n_A^0 n_B^0 e^{-(m_A+m_B) g z / k_B T} ]`, where `m_i` are particle masses, `a'_{ij}` are per-particle van der Waals parameters, `g` is gravity, `T` is temperature, `k_B` is the Boltzmann constant, and `n_i^0` are the number densities at `z=0` determined by the total number of particles `N_i`.
