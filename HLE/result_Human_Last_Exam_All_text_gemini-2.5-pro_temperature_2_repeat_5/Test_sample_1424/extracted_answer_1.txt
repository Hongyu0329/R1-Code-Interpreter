The functional dependence of the bare Green's function $G_0(k, \omega)$ on the single-particle energy eigenvalues $\epsilon_k$ is given by:

$G_0(k, \omega) = \frac{1}{\hbar\omega - \epsilon_k + i\delta \cdot \text{sgn}(\epsilon_k - E_F)}$

where $\hbar\omega$ is the energy variable, $E_F$ is the Fermi energy, and $i\delta$ is an infinitesimal imaginary term that specifies the analytic properties according to the Feynman prescription.
