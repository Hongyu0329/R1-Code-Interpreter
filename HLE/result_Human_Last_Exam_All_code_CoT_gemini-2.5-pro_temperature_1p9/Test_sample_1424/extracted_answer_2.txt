The functional dependence of the bare Green's function $G_0$ on the single-particle energy $\epsilon_k$ is given by the formula $G_0(k, \omega) = \frac{1}{\omega - \epsilon_k + i\eta \cdot \text{sgn}(\epsilon_k - \epsilon_F)}$. This means the sign of the imaginary infinitesimal term $i\eta$ depends on whether the energy eigenvalue $\epsilon_k$ is above (for particles) or below (for holes) the Fermi energy $\epsilon_F$.
