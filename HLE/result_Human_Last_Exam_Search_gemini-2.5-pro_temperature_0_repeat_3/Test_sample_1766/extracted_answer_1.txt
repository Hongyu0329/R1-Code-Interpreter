The common eigenvalues for $J^2$ and $J_z$ are:
For the state with total angular momentum quantum number $j = 3/2$:
The eigenvalue of $J^2$ is $\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$.
The eigenvalues of $J_z$ are $\frac{3}{2}\hbar, \frac{1}{2}\hbar, -\frac{1}{2}\hbar, -\frac{3}{2}\hbar$.

For the state with total angular momentum quantum number $j = 1/2$:
The eigenvalue of $J^2$ is $\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$.
The eigenvalues of $J_z$ are $\frac{1}{2}\hbar, -\frac{1}{2}\hbar$.
