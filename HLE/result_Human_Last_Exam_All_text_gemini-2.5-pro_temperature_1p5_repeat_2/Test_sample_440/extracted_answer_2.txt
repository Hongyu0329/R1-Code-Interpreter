The density profile `ρ(z)` is given by the function: `ρ(z) = m_A C_{A,0} e^{-m_A gz / (k_B T)} \left( 1 + \frac{2}{k_B T N_{Av}^2} [ a_{AA} C_{A,0} e^{-m_A gz / (k_B T)} + a_{AB} C_{B,0} e^{-m_B gz / (k_B T)} ] \right) + m_B C_{B,0} e^{-m_B gz / (k_B T)} \left( 1 + \frac{2}{k_B T N_{Av}^2} [ a_{BB} C_{B,0} e^{-m_B gz / (k_B T)} + a_{AB} C_{A,0} e^{-m_A gz / (k_B T)} ] \right)`, where `m_i = M_i / N_{Av}` and `C_{i,0} = \frac{N_i m_i g}{A k_B T (1 - \exp(-m_i g H / (k_B T)))}`.
