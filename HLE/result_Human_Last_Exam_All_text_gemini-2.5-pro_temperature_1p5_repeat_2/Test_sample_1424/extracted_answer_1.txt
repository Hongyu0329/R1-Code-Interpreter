The functional dependence of the bare Green's function in frequency-momentum space, $G_0(\mathbf{k}, \omega)$, on the single-particle energy eigenvalue $\epsilon_k$ is given by the expression $G_0(\mathbf{k}, \omega) = \frac{1}{\omega - \epsilon_k + i\eta \cdot \text{sgn}(\epsilon_k - \epsilon_F)}$, where $\epsilon_k$ sets the real part of the pole location.
