The bare Green's function in frequency-momentum space, $G_0(k, \omega)$, is a rational function whose denominator is linear in the single-particle energy eigenvalue $\epsilon_k$. Specifically, the functional dependence is of the form $G_0(k, \omega) = \frac{1}{\omega - \epsilon_k + i\eta \cdot \text{sgn}(\epsilon_k-\mu)}$, where $\epsilon_k$ determines the position of the pole on the real frequency axis.
