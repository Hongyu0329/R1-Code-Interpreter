The small molecules that will form some kind of protein-ligand interactions with the chemical represented by ChEMBL ID: CHEMBL4803817 are other angiotensin II receptor blockers (ARBs). The primary protein target is the Angiotensin II receptor type 1 (AT1R). Examples of such small molecules include Losartan, Candesartan, Irbesartan, Olmesartan, Telmisartan, Azilsartan, and Eprosartan.
