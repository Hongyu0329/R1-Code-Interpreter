
**Function f:**
Let $A$ be the formula $1 \multimap \bot$. The function $f$ maps a natural number $w$ to the formula $f(w)$ defined as:
$$
f(w) = A^w = \begin{cases} 1 & \text{if } w = 0 \\ \underbrace{A \otimes \dots \otimes A}_{w \text{ times}} & \text{if } w > 0 \end{cases}
$$

**Formula C:**
Let $A$ be the formula $1 \multimap \bot$. The formula $C$ is defined as:
$$
C(W, m, b) = (A^b)^m = \underbrace{A^b \otimes \dots \otimes A^b}_{m \text{ times}}
$$
where $A^b$ is the $b$-th tensor power of $A$.
