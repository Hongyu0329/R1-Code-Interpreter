**Basque** and **Tagalog**. In Basque, "mom" is *ama* and "dad" is *aita*. In Tagalog, the informal word for "mom" is *mama* and a formal word for "dad" is *tatay*. The words for "broom" (*erratza* in Basque and *walis* in Tagalog) are not similar. There is no evidence of direct historical contact between the Basque people and the people of the Philippines.
