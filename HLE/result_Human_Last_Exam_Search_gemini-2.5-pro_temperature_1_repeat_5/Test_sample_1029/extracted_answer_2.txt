The Poynting vector $\vec{S}$ at a radial distance $r$ from the axis is:
1.  **Inside the rod ($r \leq R$):**
    $$ \vec{S} = -\frac{E \rho v r}{2} \hat{r} $$
2.  **Outside the rod ($r > R$):**
    $$ \vec{S} = -\frac{E \rho v R^2}{2r} \hat{r} $$
where $\hat{r}$ is the unit vector in the radial direction.
