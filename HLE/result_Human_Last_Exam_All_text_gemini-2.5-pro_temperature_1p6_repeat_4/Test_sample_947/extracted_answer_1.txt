The magnetic field `H(x, z)` is a vector with x and z components, `H = H_x x̂ + H_z ẑ`. The expressions for these components are:
$H_x(x, z) = \frac{\pi^2 H_0 w^2}{D^2} \frac{1 - \cosh\left(\frac{2\pi x}{D}\right)\cos\left(\frac{2\pi z}{D}\right)}{\left[\cosh\left(\frac{2\pi x}{D}\right) - \cos\left(\frac{2\pi z}{D}\right)\right]^2}$
$H_z(x, z) = H_a - \frac{\pi^2 H_0 w^2}{D^2} \frac{\sinh\left(\frac{2\pi x}{D}\right)\sin\left(\frac{2\pi z}{D}\right)}{\left[\cosh\left(\frac{2\pi x}{D}\right) - \cos\left(\frac{2\pi z}{D}\right)\right]^2}$
