The common eigenvalues are found for two distinct sets of states corresponding to total angular momentum quantum numbers $j=3/2$ and $j=1/2$. The eigenvalues are given as pairs (eigenvalue of $J^2$, eigenvalue of $J_z$).

For the $j=3/2$ state, the pairs are:
($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{3}{2}\hbar$), ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{1}{2}\hbar$), ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{1}{2}\hbar$), ($\frac{15}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{3}{2}\hbar$).

For the $j=1/2$ state, the pairs are:
($\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$, $\frac{1}{2}\hbar$), ($\frac{3}{4}\hbar^2$, $-\frac{1}{2}\hbar$).
