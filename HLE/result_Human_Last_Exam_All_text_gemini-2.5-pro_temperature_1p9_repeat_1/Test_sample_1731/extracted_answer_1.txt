
Equilibrium Mean Energy:
$U = \sum_j \frac{g_j \epsilon_j}{e^{\epsilon_j/k_B T} - 1}$

Equilibrium Entropy:
$S = k_B \sum_j g_j \left[ \frac{\epsilon_j/k_B T}{e^{\epsilon_j/k_B T} - 1} - \ln(1 - e^{-\epsilon_j/k_B T}) \right]$

where $T$ is the equilibrium temperature, $k_B$ is the Boltzmann constant, and the system has $g_j$ quantum states at each energy level $\epsilon_j$.
