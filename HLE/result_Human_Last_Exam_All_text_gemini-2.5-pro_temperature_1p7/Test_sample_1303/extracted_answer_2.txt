The chemical `CHEMBL4803817` is the protein target SARS-CoV-2 main protease (Mpro). Small molecules that form protein-ligand interactions with it are typically inhibitors. Some of the most notable examples include:
1.  **Nirmatrelvir** (active component of Paxlovid)
2.  **Ensitrelvir** (the drug Xocova)
3.  **PF-00835231** (a clinical candidate)
4.  **GC-376** (a veterinary antiviral drug)
5.  **Boceprevir** (a repurposed Hepatitis C drug)
6.  **Telaprevir** (a repurposed Hepatitis C drug)
7.  **Carmofur** (a repurposed anticancer drug)
