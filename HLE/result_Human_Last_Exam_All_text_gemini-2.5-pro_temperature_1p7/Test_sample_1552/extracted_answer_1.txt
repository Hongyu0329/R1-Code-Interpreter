The second coefficient in the heat kernel expansion is the Seeley-DeWitt coefficient $a_2(x, D^2)$. For a massless gauged Dirac spinor field in four dimensions, its expression is given by the local geometric invariant:
$a_2(x, D^2) = \frac{1}{360} ( 7 R_{\mu\nu\rho\sigma}R^{\mu\nu\rho\sigma} - 26 R_{\mu\nu}R^{\mu\nu} + 5 R^2 ) + \frac{1}{15}\nabla^2 R - \frac{1}{3} \mathrm{tr}_{gauge}(F_{\mu\nu}F^{\mu\nu})$.
