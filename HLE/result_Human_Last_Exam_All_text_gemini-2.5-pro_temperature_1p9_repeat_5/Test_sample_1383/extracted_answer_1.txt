The achieved doubling rate is:
$W = \dfrac{1}{4}\ln\left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right) + \dfrac{3}{4}\ln\left(\dfrac{3}{4}\right)$

The decrease in the doubling rate is:
$\Delta W = W^* - W = \left( \dfrac{1}{2}\ln(2) + \dfrac{1}{2}\ln\left(\dfrac{2}{3}\right) \right) - \left( \dfrac{1}{4}\ln\left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right) + \dfrac{3}{4}\ln\left(\dfrac{3}{4}\right) \right)$
which simplifies to:
$\Delta W = \dfrac{11}{4}\ln(2) - \dfrac{3}{2}\ln(3)$
