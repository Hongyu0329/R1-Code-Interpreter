The second heat kernel coefficient $a_2(x, D^2)$ for the operator $P=D^2$ of a massless gauged Dirac spinor field is given by the formula:
$$a_2(x, D^2) = \text{tr}_{S \otimes G}\left[ \frac{1}{2}E^2 + \frac{R}{6}E + \frac{1}{12}\Omega_{\mu\nu}\Omega^{\mu\nu} + \frac{1}{180}(5R^2 - 2R_{\mu\nu}R^{\mu\nu} + 2R_{\mu\nu\rho\sigma}R^{\mu\nu\rho\sigma})\mathbb{1} \right]$$
where the trace is over the spinor and gauge group representation spaces, and the relevant operators are defined as:
*   Endomorphism $E$: $E = \frac{R}{4}\mathbb{1} + \frac{ig}{2} \sigma^{\mu\nu} \otimes F_{\mu\nu}$
*   Curvature $\Omega_{\mu\nu}$: $\Omega_{\mu\nu} = \frac{1}{2}R_{\mu\nu ab}\sigma^{ab} \otimes \mathbb{1}_G + ig \mathbb{1}_S \otimes F_{\mu\nu}$

Here, $R$, $R_{\mu\nu}$, $R_{\mu\nu\rho\sigma}$ are the scalar, Ricci, and Riemann curvature tensors, $F_{\mu\nu}$ is the gauge field strength tensor, $\sigma^{\mu\nu} = \frac{1}{2}[\gamma^\mu, \gamma^\nu]$ are the spinor generators, and $g$ is the gauge coupling constant.
