
**Function f:**
Let A be the formula `1 \multimap \bot`.
The function `f` maps a natural number `w` to the formula:
`f(w) = A^{\otimes w}` (the formula `A` tensored with itself `w` times). For `w=0`, `f(0) = 1`.

**Formula C:**
Let A be the formula `1 \multimap \bot` and B be the formula `\bot`.
Define `Fill_b = A^{\otimes b} \multimap B`.
The formula `C(W, m, b)` is defined as a nested implication:
`C = Fill_b \multimap (Fill_b \multimap \dots \multimap (Fill_b \multimap B^{\otimes m})\dots)`
where the formula `Fill_b` appears `m` times as the hypothesis.
