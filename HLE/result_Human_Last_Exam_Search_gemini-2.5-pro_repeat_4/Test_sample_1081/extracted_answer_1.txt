The exact number of $\mathbb{F}_q$-rational maximal tori of a reductive group $G$ of type $E_8$ over the finite field $\mathbb{F}_q$ is given by the formula:
$$ \frac{q^{120} (q^2-1) (q^8-1) (q^{12}-1) (q^{14}-1) (q^{18}-1) (q^{20}-1) (q^{24}-1) (q^{30}-1)}{(q-1)^8} $$
This can also be written as:
$$ q^{120} \prod_{d \in \{2, 8, 12, 14, 18, 20, 24, 30\}} (1+q+q^2+\dots+q^{d-1}) $$
