Inside the rod ($r \leq R$): $ \vec{S} = \frac{\rho^2 v r^2}{4 \epsilon_0} \hat{z} - \frac{E \rho v r}{2} \hat{r} $.
Outside the rod ($r > R$): $ \vec{S} = \frac{\rho^2 v R^4}{4 \epsilon_0 r^2} \hat{z} - \frac{E \rho v R^2}{2r} \hat{r} $.
Here, $\hat{z}$ is the unit vector along the axis in the direction of motion, and $\hat{r}$ is the unit vector pointing radially outward from the axis.
