The one-step transformation is $p_i(t+1) = \frac{1}{2}p_{i-1}(t) + \frac{1}{2}p_{i+1}(t)$.
The transition matrix is $\hat{A}_{ij} = \frac{1}{2}(\delta_{i,j+1} + \delta_{i,j-1})$, where indices are modulo N.
The eigenvectors have components $(\vec{v}_n)_j = e^{ijk_n}$ with eigenvalues $\lambda_n = \cos(k_n) = \cos(\frac{2\pi n}{N})$ for $n=0, \dots, N-1$.
The largest eigenvalue is $\lambda_0=1$. The second-largest eigenvalue is $\lambda_1 = \cos(\frac{2\pi}{N})$.
The rate of relaxation is given by the spectral gap, $1-\lambda_1 = 1 - \cos\left(\frac{2\pi}{N}\right) = 2\sin^2\left(\frac{\pi}{N}\right)$.
