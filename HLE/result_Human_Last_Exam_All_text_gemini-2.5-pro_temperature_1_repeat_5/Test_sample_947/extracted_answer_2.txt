The magnetic field is given by its components $H_x(x, z)$ and $H_z(x, z)$:
$H_x(x, z) = - \frac{4\pi^2 w^2}{D^2} H_a \tanh\left(\frac{H_a}{H_0}\right) \text{sgn}(x) \sin\left(\frac{2\pi z}{D}\right) \exp\left(-\frac{2\pi |x|}{D}\right)$
$H_z(x, z) = H_a \left[ 1 - \frac{4\pi^2 w^2}{D^2} \tanh\left(\frac{H_a}{H_0}\right) \cos\left(\frac{2\pi z}{D}\right) \exp\left(-\frac{2\pi |x|}{D}\right) \right]$
where $H_0 = \frac{J_c d}{\pi}$.
