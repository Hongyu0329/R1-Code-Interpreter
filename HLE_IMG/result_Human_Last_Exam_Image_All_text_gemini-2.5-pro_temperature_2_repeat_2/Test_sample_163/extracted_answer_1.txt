The products A and B are the two regioisomers formed from the 1,2-difunctionalization of the styrene double bond with a benzoyloxy group and a tert-butoxy group. Their structures are:
**Product 1:** 2-(tert-butoxy)-1-phenylethyl benzoate (`PhCH(OBz)CH2OtBu`)
**Product 2:** 2-benzoyloxy-1-(tert-butoxy)-1-phenylethane (`PhCH(OtBu)CH2OBz`)
