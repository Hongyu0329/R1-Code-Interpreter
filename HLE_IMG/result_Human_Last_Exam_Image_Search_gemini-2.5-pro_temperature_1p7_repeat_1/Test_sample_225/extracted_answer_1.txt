The reaction of tris(2,3-dimethoxyphenyl)methylium ion with 0.1 M HCl under reflux for 12 hours results in the cleavage of all six methyl ether bonds. Compound A is **tris(2,3-dihydroxyphenyl)methylium ion**. The structure is a central carbocation bonded to three 2,3-dihydroxyphenyl (catechol) rings.
