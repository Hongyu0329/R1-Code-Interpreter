(b) No, there is no significant risk of Lyme disease transmission from this species. The American Dog Tick is not a primary vector for Lyme disease, which is transmitted by the Black-legged Tick (*Ixodes scapularis*). However, the American Dog Tick can transmit other diseases such as Rocky Mountain Spotted Fever.
