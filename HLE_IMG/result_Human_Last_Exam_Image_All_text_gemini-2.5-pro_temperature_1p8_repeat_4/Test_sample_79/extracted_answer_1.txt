The square structure shown in the image is an archaeological site known as **El Cuadrado de Ojinaga** (The Ojinaga Square). It is located in Chihuahua, Mexico.

Yes, it is considered an **ancient structure and a historic landmark**. Its exact origin is debated by archaeologists, with theories suggesting it is either a pre-Hispanic ceremonial enclosure from the La Junta culture or an early Spanish colonial presidio (fort).
