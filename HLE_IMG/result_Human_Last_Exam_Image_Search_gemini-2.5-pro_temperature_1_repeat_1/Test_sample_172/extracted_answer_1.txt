
**Formulation of Total Power Delivered:**
The total power delivered to the grid ($P_{\text{grid}}$) is given by the formula:
$P_{\text{grid}} = (P^{0}_{\text{wind}} + P_{\text{wave}} + P_{c}) - 0.01 \times (P^{0}_{\text{wind}} + P_{\text{wave}} + P_{c})^2$

**Optimal Compensation Power ($P{c}^{\text{opt}}$):**
To meet the grid demand of at least 12 MW while minimizing losses, the optimal compensation power is:
$P{c}^{\text{opt}} = -1.055 , \text{MW}$

**Total Power Delivered to the Grid:**
With the optimal compensation, the total power delivered to the grid is:
$P_{\text{grid}} = 12.0 , \text{MW}$
