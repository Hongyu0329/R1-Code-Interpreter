The two major products, A and B, are the constitutional isomers formed by the addition of the tert-butoxy group and the benzoyloxy group across the double bond of styrene.

**Compound A:** 1-phenyl-2-(tert-butoxy)ethyl benzoate
```
      O-C(=O)Ph
     /
Ph--CH--CH₂--O-C(CH₃)₃
```

**Compound B:** 2-phenyl-2-(tert-butoxy)ethyl benzoate
```
      O-C(CH₃)₃
     /
Ph--CH--CH₂--O-C(=O)Ph
```
