A molecule with the IUPAC name 3-hydroxy-2-(pyridin-2-yl)isoindoline-1-carbonitrile. It consists of a benzene ring fused to a five-membered isoindoline ring. The nitrogen atom of the isoindoline ring is attached to a 2-pyridyl group. The carbon atom at position 1 of the isoindoline ring is bonded to a cyano group. The carbon atom at position 3 is bonded to a hydroxyl group.
