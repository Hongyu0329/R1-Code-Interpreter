Compound C is a polyhydroxy-triphenylmethane dye that exists as a neutral zwitterion with the molecular formula C23H23NO8. Its structure consists of a central carbon atom bonded to two 2,4,6-trihydroxyphenyl rings and one 4-(diethylamino)-2,6-dihydroxyphenyl ring, adopting a quinone-methide resonance form.
