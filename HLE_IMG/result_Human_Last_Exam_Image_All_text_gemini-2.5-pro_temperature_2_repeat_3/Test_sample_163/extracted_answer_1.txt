The two major products A and B are the constitutional isomers resulting from the oxidative difunctionalization of the styrene double bond. Their structures are:
**Product 1:** 2-(tert-butoxy)-1-phenylethyl benzoate
`Ph-CH(OOCPh)-CH₂-OtBu`
**Product 2:** 2-benzoyloxy-1-(tert-butoxy)-1-phenylethane
`Ph-CH(OtBu)-CH₂-OOCPh`
