A and B are the two constitutional isomers: 2-(tert-butoxy)-1-phenylethyl benzoate and 2-benzoyloxy-1-(tert-butoxy)-1-phenylethane.
Their structures are:
**A**:
```
      O-C(=O)Ph
      |
Ph - CH - CH2 - O - C(CH3)3
```
**B**:
```
      O-C(CH3)3
      |
Ph - CH - CH2 - O - C(=O)Ph
```
*(where Ph represents a phenyl group, C₆H₅)*
