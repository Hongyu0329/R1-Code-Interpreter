The products A and B are the two regioisomers formed from the 1,2-difunctionalization of styrene:
**2-(tert-butoxy)-1-phenylethyl benzoate** and **2-(tert-butoxy)-2-phenylethyl benzoate**.
The structures are:
**Product 1:**
```
        O   
       //
    O--C--Ph
    |
Ph--CH--CH2--O--C(CH3)3 
```

**Product 2:**
```
        O--C(CH3)3
        |
Ph--CH--CH2--O--C--Ph
               //
               O
```
