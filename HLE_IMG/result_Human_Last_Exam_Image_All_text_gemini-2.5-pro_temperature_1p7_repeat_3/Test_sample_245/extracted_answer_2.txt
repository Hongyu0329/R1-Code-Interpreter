(b) No, there is no significant risk of Lyme disease transmission from this species of tick. The primary vector for Lyme disease is the black-legged tick (*Ixodes scapularis*), not the American Dog Tick. However, the American Dog Tick can transmit other serious diseases, such as Rocky Mountain Spotted Fever.
