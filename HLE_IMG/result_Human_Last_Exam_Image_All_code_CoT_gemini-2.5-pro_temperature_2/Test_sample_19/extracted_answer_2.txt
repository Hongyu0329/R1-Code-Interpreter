2. The home key is C# minor. The modulation is to E minor, which is the minor mediant (iii). This is a common Romantic-era technique involving mode mixture. Instead of modulating to the brighter relative major (E Major, the III chord), Beethoven chooses the minor version to maintain the piece's somber mood while still moving to a closely related key.
