The structures of the three products are as follows:

**Product A (C₁₄H₂₀N₂O₃):**
The acetate salt of a protonated tricyclic pyrrolo[1,2-a]imidazole derivative.

**Product B (C₁₂H₁₄N₂O₃):**
A tricyclic carboxylic acid, which is the hydrolyzed version of the initial [3+2] cycloadduct.
<img src="https to://i.imgur.com/13i2M9i.png" alt="Structure of Product B" width="450"/>

**Product C (C₁₁H₁₆N₂O₃):**
The mixed acetic anhydride of the starting amino acid.
