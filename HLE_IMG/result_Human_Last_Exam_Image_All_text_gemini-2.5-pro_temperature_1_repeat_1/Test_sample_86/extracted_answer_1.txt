The predicted energy spectrum is that of a harmonic oscillator with a renormalized frequency $\omega'$, given by $E_n = \hbar \omega' (n+1/2)$, where $(\omega')^2 = \omega_0^2 + \frac{u\hbar}{4m^2\omega_0}$. Therefore, the energy spectrum is $E_n = \hbar \left( \omega_0^2 + \frac{u\hbar}{4m^2\omega_0} \right)^{1/2} \left( n + \frac{1}{2} \right)$.
