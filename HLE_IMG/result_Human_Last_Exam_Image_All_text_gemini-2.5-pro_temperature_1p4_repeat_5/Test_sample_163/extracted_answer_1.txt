The two major products, A and B, are the constitutional isomers resulting from the 1,2-difunctionalization of styrene. They are **2-(tert-butoxy)-1-phenylethyl benzoate** and **2-(tert-butoxy)-2-phenylethyl benzoate**.

**Product A:**
```
      OCOC₆H₅
     /
C₆H₅-CH - CH₂-O-C(CH₃)₃
```
**Product B:**
```
      O-C(CH₃)₃
     /
C₆H₅-CH - CH₂-OCOC₆H₅
```
